Modernize without friction

From legacy systems to lasting agility

Legacy systems are expensive to maintain and difficult to scale. With the launch of the MongoDB Application Modernization Platform (AMP), enterprises can transform complex, outdated systems into scalable, modern services. AMP can help you modernize up to three times faster than traditional methods, so you can pay down technical debt and free up IT spend for innovation and faster time to market.
Talk to Us

Unleash the potential of your app stack

MongoDB AMP fuses AI, a proven delivery framework, and expert engineers to modernize your business-critical applications. It's tools, technique, and talent.

Transform your apps and your data

Unlike traditional lift and shift migrations, AMP provides complete, full-stack application transformation, modernizing both application logic and data architecture rather than migrating technical debt from one technology to another.

Test and modernize with confidence

MongoDB AMP empowers organizations to validate transformations with comprehensive test suites, minimizing disruption and ensuring that what's working remains intact while modernizing brittle code and outdated infrastructure.
