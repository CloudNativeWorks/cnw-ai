Chirantan CJ Desai, President & CEO

LinkedIn | X/Twitter

Chirantan CJ Desai is the President and Chief Executive Officer of MongoDB, also serving on the companys Board of Directors. Under his leadership, MongoDB is advancing its position as the default modern database platform powering AI, cloud, and real-time applications.

CJ brings more than 25 years of experience leading global product and engineering organizations across cloud infrastructure, enterprise software, and cybersecurity. Through his experiences, hes developed a deep understanding of growth at scale for both technology innovation and GTM execution. Before joining MongoDB, he served as President of Product and Engineering at Cloudflare and as President and Chief Operating Officer at ServiceNow, where he helped grow revenue organically from $1.5 billion to more than $10 billion. CJ previously held senior roles at EMC and Symantec and started his technology career at Oracle. He also serves on the Board of Directors at Cribl.

CJ earned a Masters in Computer Science and an MBA from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, where he is a recipient of the Distinguished Alumni Achievement Award.

Cedric Pech, President of Field Operations

LinkedIn

Cedric is the President of Field Operations of MongoDB. Since joining, Cedric has rapidly scaled MongoDBs go to market (GTM) team, expanded into new global markets, and transformed its business into a consumption-driven GTM model. Cedric previously served as Chief Revenue Officer, where he more than tripled MongoDBs ARR. Prior to MongoDB, he was Senior VP of Worldwide Sales at Fuze, a cloud communications and collaboration platform, and has also held VP and GM roles for publicly-traded software companies BladeLogic, BMC Software, and BazaarVoice. Cedric completed his Class Prparatoire at Lyce Bois Fleury Grenoble and received his M.B.A. from Montpellier Business School.

Mike Berry, Chief Financial Officer

LinkedIn

Mike is the Chief Financial Officer of MongoDB. He brings more than 25 years of experience in finance, operations, and general management across the technology industry. Mike oversees MongoDBs global finance organization, including financial strategy, planning and analysis, accounting, and investor relations. Prior to MongoDB, Mike has served as a CFO at seven companies including NetApp, McAfee, FireEye, Informatica, IO (now Iron Mountain), SolarWinds, and i2 Technologies. Mike holds a bachelors degree in finance from Augsburg College and an MBA from the University of St. Thomas. He also serves on the board of directors for Rapid7 and Calix.

Paul Capombassis, Chief Revenue Officer

LinkedIn

Paul Capombassis is the Chief Revenue Officer of MongoDB. Paul joined MongoDB in 2017 to lead its North America business and has played a pivotal role in the companys growth journey, having achieved over 13x revenue growth in the region. Over his tenure, Pauls role has expanded to include broader responsibilities, covering EMEA, Sales Development, Inside Sales & Enablement. He is deeply committed to nurturing internal talent, and nearly 90% of his leadership team comprises of individuals promoted under his guidance, showcasing his ability to nurture and develop his teams. Paul has a long-standing history of achieving significant growth and fostering innovation, and before joining MongoDB, he held leadership roles at prestigious companies such as Splunk, BMC, and AppDynamics. He holds a B.A. in History from Bishops University.

Harsha Jalihal, Chief People Officer

LinkedIn

Harsha Jalihal is the Chief People Officer of MongoDB. Under her leadership, MongoDB has more than doubled its workforce across dozens of countries and was recognized as a Most-Loved Workplace by Newsweeks Excellence Index 2024. Previously Harsha was Vice President of HR at Unilever, where she was responsible for end-to-end HR strategy and operations for their U.S. business, co-created Unilevers future of work strategy, and worked on a part-time secondment to the World Economic Forum where she contributed to their thought leadership on the future of work. Prior to this, she spent 10 years at Cognizant in various HR and business roles, laying the early foundations of their talent acquisition, talent management, reward management, M&A and employee relations practices. Harsha was recognized as Crain's New York Notable Women of Talent for 2020.

Jim Scharf, Chief Technology Officer

LinkedIn

Jim is the Chief Technology Officer of MongoDB. He leads the companys Product & Technology organization, driving product development and engineering strategy for MongoDBs products and services. Jim comes to MongoDB with over 17 years of experience at Amazon Web Services (AWS). As one of AWSs first employees, he was the founding owner of six services, served as General Manager of the Amazon DynamoDB and AWS Identity businesses, and was most recently Vice President of AWS Identity. While there, Jim was an author on 20 foundational U.S. patents in the area of cloud computing. Previously, he worked in numerous software engineering and technology consulting roles. He holds a Bachelors in Engineering from Rutgers University.

Olivier Zieleniecki, Global Vice President of Worldwide Partners

LinkedIn

Olivier Zieleniecki is the Global Vice President of Worldwide Partners at MongoDB. He has been with MongoDB since 2016 and spent the first five years in senior direct sales leadership positions within North America where he achieved over 10x revenue growth. During his tenure, Olivier took on global leadership of MongoDBs cloud partnership business, significantly expanding our co-sell initiatives and partnerships with the hyperscalers. Today, cloud partners touch over 50% of MongoDBs new business. Olivier has a long-standing history of achieving high revenue growth at fast pace software companies. Prior to MongoDB, Olivier held a number of sales roles at enterprise technology companies including ClearSlide and ClickFox. He is also a Y.P.O member. Olivier holds a B.A. and an M.B.A. from Southeastern Louisiana University.

Andrew Stephens, General Counsel

LinkedIn

Andrew is the General Counsel of MongoDB. Since 2013, he has shepherded the company through major go-to-market and commercial transformations, multiple acquisitions, and a successful IPO. Andrew is one of the longest serving leaders at MongoDB and has helped lead the Company through its rapid evolution from startup to member of the NASDAQ 100. Prior to MongoDB, Andrew was the Managing Counsel at LinkedIn where he focused on technology transactions, strategic partnerships, and revenue generating contracts. He was LinkedIns second lawyer hired and guided the company through its hyper-growth phase and IPO. Earlier, Andrew served as Assistant General Counsel at Logitech, where he joined after advising the company as outside counsel while working at Wilson Sonsini. Andrew started his legal career at Lawson Lundell in Vancouver, Canada after receiving his L.L.B. from Dalhousie University. He also holds a Bachelor of Commerce degree from the University of British Columbia.

Deepa Gopinath, Chief Information Officer

LinkedIn
